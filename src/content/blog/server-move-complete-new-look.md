All About Astro.com started more than a decade ago. Hard to believe!

Over recent years, my involvement in the hobby has slowed substantially. Life just does that, sometimes. But for the longest time, I had been tired of the tedium of maintaining a webpage using a very limited tool set, whereas site graphics and design was exclusively by Yours Truly and the programming was just old fashioned HTML. And considering the cost of being hosted on my old server, I had been meaning to move hosts to something more economical as well as more user friendly. There's just no reason to keep reinventing the wheel anymore.

So, the server move is complete. While the webpage will remain under construction for quite a while, no doubt, I can now start importing my old site images, advice, and blog to this new, more user-friendly format. The goal? To get reinvigorated to do more astronomy and to pass on those experiences with my readers.

I sincerely hope you will grow to like the NEW All About Astro.com.

- jay
