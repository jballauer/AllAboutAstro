The Markarian's Chain is a striking curved line of galaxies — including M84, M86, and several NGC members — at the core of the Virgo Cluster, in the constellation Virgo. The cluster lies roughly 53–60 million light-years away, and the visible chain of galaxies spans about 1.5 degrees of sky, a rich hunting ground for observing many galaxies in a single field of view.
