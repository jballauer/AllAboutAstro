Location: The Ballauer Observatory near Azle, Texas. Transparency 4.8 mag zenithal, seeing 7/10, temperature 38°F, camera cooled to -25°C.

Reprocessed in May 2013 using Dark frame calibration, flat fields, registration, and Winsor Rejection combine in PixInsight, followed by final HaRGB combine, deblooming, color balance, and selective sharpening in Photoshop CS6.
