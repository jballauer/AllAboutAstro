Barnard 33, the Horsehead Nebula, is a dense cloud of dark, cool gas and dust silhouetted against the bright background emission nebula IC 434, near the star Alnitak in the constellation Orion. It lies about 1,375 light-years away and spans roughly 6 by 4 arcminutes — one of the most recognizable dark nebulae in the sky, though faint enough to be a difficult visual target. Approximate coordinates: RA 05h 40m 59s, Dec -02° 27′ 30″.
