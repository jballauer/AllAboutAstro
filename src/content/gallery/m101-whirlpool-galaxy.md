M101 is more commonly cataloged as the Pinwheel Galaxy (the name "Whirlpool" is usually reserved for the similarly-shaped M51). It is a grand-design face-on spiral in the constellation Ursa Major, lying about 21 million light-years away. It shines at an apparent magnitude of 7.9 and spans roughly 28.8 by 26.9 arcminutes.
