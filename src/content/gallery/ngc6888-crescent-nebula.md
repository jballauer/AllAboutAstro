NGC 6888, the Crescent Nebula, is a bubble-shaped emission nebula formed by fast stellar wind from a rare Wolf-Rayet star colliding with material the star shed earlier in its life, in the constellation Cygnus. It lies about 5,000 light-years away, shines around magnitude 7.4, and spans roughly 20 by 10 arcminutes. Approximate coordinates: RA 20h 12m, Dec +38° 21′.
