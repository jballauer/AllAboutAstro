The Rho Ophiuchi cloud complex, together with the nearby red supergiant Antares, forms one of the most colorful regions in the sky, straddling the border of Ophiuchus and Scorpius. The Rho Ophiuchi dark and reflection nebulae lie about 460 light-years away, while Antares itself (apparent magnitude 1.06) lies around 550 light-years away. The full complex of dust, reflection nebulae, and star clusters spans several degrees of sky.
