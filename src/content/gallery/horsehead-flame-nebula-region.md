The Horsehead Nebula (Barnard 33) and Flame Nebula (NGC 2024) sit side by side near the star Alnitak in Orion's belt. B33 is a dark dust cloud silhouetted against the glow of emission nebula IC 434, about 1,375 light-years away, spanning roughly 6 by 4 arcminutes. NGC 2024 is an emission nebula at a similar distance, spanning about 30 by 30 arcminutes.
