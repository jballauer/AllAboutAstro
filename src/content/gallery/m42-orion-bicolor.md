M42, the Orion Nebula, is a diffuse emission and reflection nebula and one of the closest large star-forming regions to Earth, in the constellation Orion. It lies approximately 1,344 light-years away, shines at an apparent magnitude of about 4.0, and spans a large 65 by 60 arcminutes — easily visible to the naked eye as the fuzzy "star" in Orion's sword. Approximate coordinates: RA 05h 35m 17s, Dec -05° 23′ 28″.
