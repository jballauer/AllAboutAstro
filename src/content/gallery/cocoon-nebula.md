The Cocoon Nebula (IC 5146) is a compact emission and reflection nebula wrapped around a young open cluster, at the end of a long dark dust trail (Barnard 168), in the constellation Cygnus. It lies about 4,000 light-years away, shines around magnitude 7.2, and spans roughly 12 by 12 arcminutes. Approximate coordinates: RA 21h 53m 24s, Dec +47° 16′.
